The Union Cabinet chaired by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has approved the Employment Linked Incentive (ELI) Scheme to support employment generation, enhance employability and social security across all sectors...

Part A: Incentive to First Time Employees

Targeting first-time employees registered with EPFO, this Part will offer one-month EPF wage up to Rs 15,000 in two installments...

Part B: Support to Employers

Employers hiring additional employees (minimum 2 or 5 depending on size) get up to Rs 3,000/month for 2 years (extended to 4 years for manufacturing sector).
